Invitation


Hydrometallurgy is an important method for extracting metals from mineral resources, and an important field of the metallurgical processing industry. In recent years, in order to meet the more stringent environmental regulation and alleviate the resource deficiency, the sustainable development of hydrometallurgy industries is facing the challenge of developing strategies for low-carbon and green utilization of low grade associated complex mineral resources.
Sponsored by the Nonferrous Metals Society of China (Nfsoc), organized by Jiangxi University of Science and Technology (JXUST) and Tsinghua University (THU), the 7th International Conference on Hydrometallurgy (ICHM) will be held in Ganzhou in Jiangxi on May 8-10, 2020. This conference aims to promote the cooperation and academic exchanges among scholars from the international hydrometallurgical community and relevant industry experts, and promote the development of hydrometallurgical process industry in China.
The conference will be mainly reflected in forms of academic achievements, papers, oral communications, and posters. The organizing committee earnestly invites experts and delegates from the hydrometallurgical industries to attend the conference. Excellent articles will be recommended to academical journals, such as Hydrometallurgy, Transactions of Nonferrous Metals Society of China and Tungsten, the rest of the accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings.
